Student Accomodation

Explore student accommodation options both on and off campus

The University offers one of the most affordable student accommodation options. Experience life in an inclusive, supportive and authentic environment with students from all over Australia and the world.

Our student residences, in Camperdown and Darlington and Camden, provide a supportive community that fosters personal and academic growth. Whether you're a current or future student, or looking for someone, you can find all the key information on each residence page, including:

  • Location
  • Costs
  • Facilities
  • Inclusions
  • Highlights
  • Testimonials
  • Room tours
Resident students can also get involved in the various sporting, cultural and wellbeing events that run year-long as part of our Intramural Program, allowing you to create memories and friendships during your time at uni. There are also off campus options and information about living costs.

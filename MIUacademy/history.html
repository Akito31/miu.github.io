About MIU University

A research-intensive public university, Myanmar Information University has 33,000 undergraduate and postgraduate students in the Software and Hardware Engineering, Data Analyst, Business IT, Cyber Security and other majors. It was built and created in 2018.

MIU is also home to world-renowned autonomous institutes the National Institute of Education, High School of International Studies and a Centre for Computer Sciences Engineering and various leading research centres such as the IT couslulting company and other BiG start up compianies.

Under the NTU Smart Campus vision, the University harnesses the power of digital technology and tech-enabled solutions to support better learning and living experiences, the discovery of new knowledge, and the sustainability of resources.

Ranked amongst the world’s top universities, the University main campus is also frequently listed among the world’s most beautiful. Known for its sustainability, MIU has achieved 100% Green Mark Platinum certification for all its eligible building projects. Apart from its main campus, MIU also has a medical campus in Kamayout, Yangon healthcare district.
